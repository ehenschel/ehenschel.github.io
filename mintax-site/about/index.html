Our Story

MinTax Group traces its roots to March 1999, when MinTax LLP was established in Almaty to provide tax and legal consulting services. In those early years of Kazakhstan's independence, businesses needed partners who understood both the emerging regulatory landscape and international best practices.

We expanded in July 2001 with the establishment of MinTax Audit LLP, adding independent audit and assurance services to our offering. This allowed us to provide truly integrated advice — combining financial, tax, and legal perspectives under one roof.

In 2011, we strengthened our legal capabilities through a close alliance with Premium Solutions, an independent law firm. This partnership brings advocacy expertise and court representation to complement our advisory services.

Today, MinTax Group serves clients across Kazakhstan from offices in Almaty, Astana, and Atyrau. Our team includes certified auditors, tax consultants, lawyers, and accountants — many holding international qualifications including ACCA, CPA, and DipIFR.
