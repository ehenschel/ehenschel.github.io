IFRS Reporting

Financial statement transformation, first-time adoption, and ongoing IFRS support. Helping companies meet international reporting requirements.

Our IFRS Services

International Financial Reporting Standards have become the global language of financial reporting. In Kazakhstan, IFRS is mandatory for listed companies, financial institutions, and many other organizations. We help companies navigate these complex requirements.

Our IFRS specialists include holders of the ACCA Diploma in IFRS (DipIFR) and professionals with extensive experience in financial statement preparation and transformation.

What We Offer

  • IFRS Transformation — Converting financial statements from local GAAP to IFRS
  • First-Time Adoption — Managing the transition to IFRS reporting (IFRS 1)
  • Consolidated Reporting — Preparation of group financial statements
  • Technical Consultations — Guidance on specific standards and complex transactions
  • Policy Development — Designing accounting policies and procedures
  • Training — IFRS training for finance teams

Complex Standards

We provide specialized guidance on standards that often present challenges:

  • IFRS 9 — Financial instruments classification, measurement, and impairment
  • IFRS 15 — Revenue from contracts with customers
  • IFRS 16 — Lease accounting and implementation
  • IAS 36 — Impairment testing of assets and CGUs
  • IFRS 3 — Business combinations and purchase price allocation
  • IAS 19 — Employee benefits and actuarial valuations

Industry-Specific Guidance

Different industries face different IFRS challenges. We have experience with:

  • Extractive Industries — IFRS 6, asset retirement obligations, production sharing
  • Financial Services — Complex financial instruments, hedge accounting
  • Construction — Revenue recognition over time, contract modifications
  • Retail — Inventory valuation, customer loyalty programs

Consolidation Services

For groups with complex structures, we provide:

  • Design of consolidation processes and templates
  • Elimination of intercompany transactions
  • Foreign currency translation (IAS 21)
  • Non-controlling interest calculations
  • Equity method accounting for associates and joint ventures

Our Approach

We believe IFRS reporting should be practical and sustainable. Our approach emphasizes:

  • Understanding your business — IFRS application depends on the substance of transactions
  • Building internal capability — We transfer knowledge, not just deliverables
  • Efficient processes — Designing sustainable reporting procedures
  • Auditor coordination — Working effectively with your external auditors
