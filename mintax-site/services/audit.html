Audit & Assurance

Independent, objective audits that meet the highest professional standards. Licensed by the Ministry of Finance and approved for KASE-listed companies.

Our Audit Services

MinTax Audit LLP provides comprehensive audit and assurance services to companies across Kazakhstan. Established in 2001, we have built a reputation for technical excellence, independence, and practical business insight.

Our audit team includes certified auditors with experience at major international firms, bringing global best practices to every engagement while understanding local regulatory requirements.

What We Offer

  • Statutory Audits — Annual audits required under Kazakhstan law for qualifying companies
  • IFRS Audits — Audits of financial statements prepared under International Financial Reporting Standards
  • Group Audits — Consolidated financial statement audits for holding structures
  • Special Purpose Audits — Audits of specific financial information for regulatory or contractual purposes
  • Agreed-Upon Procedures — Targeted engagements to examine specific accounts or transactions
  • Due Diligence — Financial due diligence for M&A transactions and investments

Industry Expertise

We have particular depth in sectors critical to Kazakhstan's economy:

  • Oil, gas, and mining (including subsoil use agreements)
  • Financial services and banking
  • Manufacturing and industrial
  • Transport and infrastructure
  • Retail and consumer goods

Our Credentials

MinTax Audit holds the necessary licenses and accreditations to serve Kazakhstan's most demanding clients:

  • Licensed by the Ministry of Finance of the Republic of Kazakhstan
  • Approved auditor for companies listed on the Kazakhstan Stock Exchange (KASE)
  • ISO 9001:2015 certified quality management system
  • Member of professional audit associations

Our Approach

We believe audit should add value beyond the opinion letter. Our approach emphasizes:

  • Early communication — We identify issues early and work with management to resolve them
  • Practical recommendations — Our management letters include actionable suggestions for improving controls
  • Continuity — Senior team members maintain direct involvement throughout the engagement
  • Efficiency — We leverage technology and careful planning to minimize disruption to your operations
