Brand Overview

MinTax Group has been a trusted partner for Kazakhstan's leading businesses since 1999. Our brand reflects our values: expertise built on trust, precision delivered with warmth, and a forward-looking approach rooted in deep experience.

Our Mission

To provide professional services that help businesses navigate complexity with confidence — combining technical excellence with genuine partnership.

Our Values

Integrity in everything we do. Excellence without arrogance. Clarity in complexity. Partnership over transaction.

Our Position

The trusted alternative to global firms — offering international standards with local expertise and personal attention.

Photography

Photography should feel authentic, professional, and warm. We favor real moments over staged corporate imagery.

Style Guidelines

✓ Do

  • Natural lighting when possible
  • Real people in authentic settings
  • Clean, uncluttered compositions
  • Warm, approachable expressions
  • Professional but not stiff
  • Kazakhstan context where relevant

✕ Don't

  • Obvious stock photography
  • Overly posed or artificial
  • Heavy filters or effects
  • Cluttered backgrounds
  • Generic "business handshake" clichés
  • Low resolution or pixelated images

Image Categories

Category Use Case Notes

Office/Environment About page, careers Real MinTax spaces, clean and modern
Client Interaction Services, case studies Meetings, collaboration, consultation
Kazakhstan Context Homepage, about Almaty/Astana skylines, local business

Voice & Tone

How we communicate is as important as what we communicate. Our voice is confident but not arrogant, expert but accessible, professional but warm.

Voice Principles

✓ We are confident

"We've helped Kazakhstan's leading businesses navigate tax complexity for over 25 years."

✕ Not arrogant

"We're the best tax consultants in the country and no one else comes close."

✓ We are expert

"The new Tax Code introduces several provisions that affect transfer pricing — here's what you need to know."

✕ Not jargon-heavy

"The amended NLA provisions pursuant to Article 12.3(b) necessitate recalibration of BEPS-aligned TP methodologies."

✓ We are warm

"We'd love to discuss how we can help. Get in touch — we're always happy to talk."

✕ Not casual

"Hey! Hit us up if you need tax stuff done. We're pretty good at it lol."

Writing Guidelines

  • Use active voice: "We prepare your returns" not "Returns are prepared by our team"
  • Be specific: "26 years of experience" not "decades of experience"
  • Lead with benefits: What does the client gain?
  • Avoid superlatives: Skip "best," "leading," "unparalleled" — let work speak
  • Keep sentences short: Aim for 15-20 words average
  • Use "you" and "we": Create direct connection with reader
