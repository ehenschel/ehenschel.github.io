Government & Public Sector

Specialized audit, compliance, and governance advisory for state enterprises, quasi-government entities, and public organizations operating under Kazakhstan's strict transparency and accountability requirements.

Industry Overview

Kazakhstan's government and public sector organizations operate under strict transparency, procurement, and financial reporting requirements. State enterprises, national holding companies, and quasi-government entities manage substantial public assets and are subject to oversight from multiple regulatory bodies, including the Accounts Committee, sector regulators, and international financial institutions.

MinTax Group provides audit, compliance, and advisory services tailored to the unique demands of the public sector. We understand the governance frameworks, budget disciplines, and accountability standards that define this environment, and we bring the technical rigor required to meet them.

How We Help

  • Public Procurement Compliance Audits — Reviewing procurement processes, tender documentation, and contract execution against the requirements of Kazakhstan's public procurement legislation
  • State Enterprise Financial Audits — Conducting statutory and IFRS audits for state-owned enterprises, national companies, and organizations with government participation
  • Governance & Internal Controls Advisory — Assessing and strengthening corporate governance structures, board effectiveness, and internal control systems in line with best practices and regulatory expectations
  • Budget Execution Audits — Evaluating the accuracy and compliance of budget execution, expenditure classification, and financial reporting against approved budget parameters
  • Anti-Corruption Compliance Programs — Designing and implementing compliance frameworks that address anti-corruption legislation, conflict-of-interest policies, and whistleblower mechanisms
  • IFRS Implementation for State Entities — Supporting the transition from national accounting standards to IFRS, including gap analysis, policy development, staff training, and first-time adoption reporting
  • Tax Advisory for Quasi-Government Organizations — Advising on the tax obligations and optimization opportunities available to entities with mixed government and commercial operations

Key Regulatory Knowledge

Serving public sector clients requires in-depth knowledge of the legal and regulatory landscape that governs government operations. Our team maintains current expertise in:

  • Public Procurement Law — Procedures, thresholds, and compliance requirements under the Law on Public Procurement, including electronic procurement platform rules
  • State Audit Requirements — External and internal audit standards applicable to state entities, including requirements of the Accounts Committee for Control over Execution of the Republican Budget
  • Samruk-Kazyna Corporate Governance Code — Governance standards, board composition requirements, and transparency obligations for entities within the Samruk-Kazyna national wealth fund portfolio
  • Budget Code Provisions — Budget planning, execution, and reporting requirements at republican and local levels
  • Anti-Corruption Legislation — Obligations under the Law on Combating Corruption, asset declaration requirements, and related compliance standards
  • Public Sector IFRS Adoption Timelines — Mandatory transition schedules, implementation guidance, and reporting requirements for government entities moving to international standards

Why MinTax

MinTax Group has established relationships with government entities and a thorough understanding of the public sector regulatory environment. Our team has direct experience working with Samruk-Kazyna portfolio companies, national holding companies, and state agencies at both the republican and regional levels.

We recognize that public sector engagements demand the highest standards of independence, confidentiality, and professional rigor.
