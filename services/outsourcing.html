Accounting & Outsourcing

Comprehensive bookkeeping, payroll, and financial reporting services. Your virtual finance department, backed by professional expertise.

Our Outsourcing Services

For many companies, maintaining a full in-house accounting function is neither practical nor cost-effective. Our outsourcing services provide access to professional accounting expertise without the overhead of a permanent finance team.

Whether you need complete bookkeeping services or support for specific functions like payroll, we tailor our services to your needs.

What We Offer

  • Bookkeeping — Day-to-day recording of transactions and maintenance of accounting records
  • Payroll Processing — Salary calculations, tax withholdings, and statutory contributions
  • Financial Reporting — Preparation of monthly, quarterly, and annual financial statements
  • Tax Compliance — Preparation and filing of tax returns and declarations
  • HR Administration — Employment documentation, leave tracking, and personnel records
  • Management Reporting — Custom reports for internal decision-making

Bookkeeping Services

Our bookkeeping services cover all aspects of day-to-day accounting:

  • Processing of invoices, payments, and receipts
  • Bank reconciliations
  • Accounts receivable and payable management
  • Fixed asset register maintenance
  • Inventory accounting
  • General ledger maintenance

Payroll Services

Payroll in Kazakhstan involves complex calculations and strict deadlines. We handle:

  • Monthly salary calculations
  • Individual income tax (IIT) withholding
  • Pension contributions (UAPF)
  • Social contributions (GFSS, OSMS, OSMC)
  • Payslip preparation
  • Statutory reporting to tax authorities

Who We Serve

Our outsourcing services are particularly well-suited for:

  • Foreign companies — Branches and representative offices of international groups
  • Start-ups — Growing companies not yet ready for a full finance team
  • Project-based businesses — Companies with fluctuating accounting workloads
  • Small to mid-sized enterprises — Organizations seeking professional support at reasonable cost

Our Approach

We believe outsourcing should feel like an extension of your team. Our approach emphasizes:

  • Dedicated contacts — You work with the same people who know your business
  • Transparent communication — Regular updates and easy access to your information
  • Scalable services — We grow with you, adding services as needed
  • Quality assurance — Senior review of all deliverables

Technology

We use modern accounting software and secure systems to ensure efficient, accurate processing. We can work with your existing systems or provide our own infrastructure, depending on your preferences.
