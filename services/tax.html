Tax Consulting

Strategic tax planning, compliance support, and dispute resolution. We help businesses navigate Kazakhstan's complex tax environment with confidence.

Our Tax Services

MinTax LLP has been providing tax consulting services since 1999, making us one of Kazakhstan's most experienced independent tax advisory firms. Our team combines deep technical knowledge with practical experience gained from hundreds of engagements across all major industries.

We were recognized as the Best Tax Consultants' Organization in Kazakhstan in 2016 — a testament to our commitment to excellence and client service.

What We Offer

  • Tax Planning & Optimization — Structuring transactions and operations to achieve tax efficiency within legal boundaries
  • Tax Compliance — Preparation and review of tax returns, ensuring accurate and timely filing
  • Tax Audit Support — Representation and assistance during tax authority audits
  • Tax Dispute Resolution — Appeals, negotiations, and litigation support for tax disputes
  • Transfer Pricing — Documentation, policy development, and defense of intercompany pricing
  • International Tax — Cross-border structuring, treaty analysis, and permanent establishment issues

Specialized Expertise

We have developed particular expertise in areas of high complexity and importance to Kazakhstan:

  • Subsoil Use Taxation — Mineral extraction tax, excess profit tax, and PSA/contract-specific regimes
  • Oil & Gas — Upstream, midstream, and downstream tax issues including customs
  • Foreign Investment — Tax implications of investing in Kazakhstan, repatriation strategies
  • Special Economic Zones — Incentives, compliance requirements, and optimization

Tax Audit Defense

When tax authorities come calling, you need experienced advocates on your side. Our tax audit defense services include:

  • Pre-audit preparation and risk assessment
  • Managing the audit process and document requests
  • Negotiating with inspectors on contentious issues
  • Drafting objections and appeals
  • Representation before appellate bodies and courts

Our Approach

We believe good tax advice requires understanding your business, not just your tax returns. Our approach emphasizes:

  • Practical solutions — Advice that works in practice, not just on paper
  • Proactive communication — We keep you informed of changes that affect your business
  • Risk transparency — We clearly explain the risks and probabilities of different positions
  • Long-term relationships — We invest in understanding your business for better ongoing advice
