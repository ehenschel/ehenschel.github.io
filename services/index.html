Our Services

Comprehensive professional services for businesses operating in Kazakhstan. From audit and tax to legal advisory and accounting support.

    Statutory audits, IFRS audits, agreed-upon procedures, and specialized assurance engagements. KASE-recognized and Ministry-licensed.
