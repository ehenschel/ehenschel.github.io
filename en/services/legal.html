Legal Services

Corporate law, M&A, dispute resolution, and regulatory compliance. Full-service legal support through our affiliated law firm, Premium Solutions.

Our Legal Services

Legal services within MinTax Group are provided through our close alliance with Premium Solutions, an independent law firm established in 2011. This partnership allows us to offer integrated advice that considers tax, accounting, and legal implications together.

Our legal team includes advocates licensed to represent clients in Kazakhstan courts, ensuring you have qualified representation when disputes arise.

What We Offer

  • Corporate Law — Company formation, governance, shareholder agreements, and corporate restructuring
  • Mergers & Acquisitions — Transaction structuring, due diligence, documentation, and closing
  • Commercial Contracts — Drafting, review, and negotiation of business agreements
  • Employment Law — Employment contracts, HR policies, terminations, and labor disputes
  • Regulatory Compliance — Licensing, permits, and regulatory approvals
  • Dispute Resolution — Litigation, arbitration, and mediation

Corporate Transactions

We support clients through all stages of corporate transactions:

  • Company Formation — Registration of LLPs, JSCs, branches, and representative offices
  • Reorganization — Mergers, spin-offs, transformations, and liquidations
  • Joint Ventures — Structuring, documentation, and governance arrangements
  • Share Transactions — Purchases, sales, and transfers of participatory interests
  • Due Diligence — Legal review of target companies and assets

Dispute Resolution

When disputes arise, our licensed advocates provide representation in:

  • Civil and commercial litigation in Kazakhstan courts
  • Administrative appeals and proceedings
  • International arbitration (ICC, LCIA, GAFTA)
  • Mediation and negotiated settlements
  • Enforcement of foreign judgments and arbitral awards

Industry Focus

We have particular experience advising clients in:

  • Oil & Gas — Subsoil use contracts, JOAs, service agreements
  • Mining — Mineral rights, licensing, and project development
  • Infrastructure — Construction contracts, PPP arrangements
  • Foreign Investment — Market entry, investment protection, repatriation

Our Approach

We believe legal advice should be practical and business-focused. Our approach emphasizes:

  • Commercial awareness — We understand business objectives, not just legal technicalities
  • Integrated advice — Legal, tax, and accounting perspectives considered together
  • Clear communication — Advice in plain language, with clear recommendations
  • Responsive service — We understand that business doesn't wait for lawyers
