Financial Services

Specialized audit, tax, and regulatory advisory for banks, insurance companies, microfinance organizations, and AIFC participants. Deep understanding of Kazakhstan's financial regulatory landscape.

Sector Overview

Kazakhstan's financial sector is regulated by the Agency for Regulation and Development of the Financial Market (ARDFM) and operates within an increasingly sophisticated framework that aligns with international standards. The sector encompasses commercial banks, insurance companies, microfinance organizations, payment service providers, and participants of the Astana International Financial Centre (AIFC).

MinTax serves financial institutions across this spectrum, providing the specialized audit, tax, and compliance services that this highly regulated sector demands. Our team understands the unique reporting requirements, capital adequacy rules, and supervisory expectations that financial institutions must navigate.

Our Services for Financial Institutions

  • Bank & Insurance Company Audits — Statutory and IFRS audits tailored to the specific accounting and disclosure requirements of financial institutions, including IFRS 9 (Financial Instruments) and IFRS 17 (Insurance Contracts)
  • Regulatory Compliance Advisory — Guidance on prudential requirements, capital adequacy calculations, liquidity ratios, and regulatory reporting obligations to the ARDFM
  • AIFC Framework Advisory — Navigating the legal and regulatory framework of the Astana International Financial Centre, including company formation, licensing, and ongoing compliance
  • IFRS Implementation for Financial Institutions — Transition planning and implementation support for complex IFRS standards applicable to banks, insurers, and investment funds
  • Tax Optimization for Banking Operations — Strategic tax planning for financial products, interbank transactions, and cross-border financial flows
  • Internal Controls Assessment — Evaluation and enhancement of internal control frameworks, risk management systems, and governance structures
  • AML/KYC Compliance Support — Advisory on anti-money laundering and know-your-customer procedures, including policy development, training, and compliance testing

Key Regulatory Knowledge

Our financial services team maintains current expertise across the regulatory landscape:

  • Central Bank / ARDFM regulations and supervisory requirements
  • AIFC rules, including the AIFC Financial Services Framework Regulations
  • Basel III/IV capital adequacy and liquidity requirements
  • Insurance solvency requirements and actuarial reserving standards
  • Payment systems regulation and electronic money rules
  • AML/CFT legislation and Financial Monitoring Agency requirements
  • Securities market regulations and KASE listing requirements

Why MinTax for Financial Services

MinTax brings a thorough understanding of both domestic and international financial regulatory frameworks. Our professionals have direct experience auditing and advising KASE-listed financial institutions, second-tier banks, insurance companies, and AIFC-regulated entities.

We combine deep technical knowledge of financial instrument accounting and regulatory capital calculations with practical experience navigating the supervisory environment. This allows us to deliver advice that meets regulatory expectations while supporting our clients' strategic objectives and operational efficiency.
