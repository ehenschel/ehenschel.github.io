Construction & Real Estate

Comprehensive tax, audit, and advisory services for development companies, construction firms, and real estate investors operating across Kazakhstan's fast-growing built environment.

Industry Overview

Kazakhstan's construction sector is one of the fastest growing in Central Asia, driven by national infrastructure development programs, urban expansion, and significant private investment in residential and commercial real estate. Major government initiatives, including the Nurly Zhol economic policy and ongoing development of the new capital, continue to fuel demand across all segments of the market.

MinTax Group advises development companies, construction firms, and real estate investors on the full range of tax, legal, and compliance challenges that arise in this dynamic and capital-intensive sector. Our team understands the unique financial structures, regulatory requirements, and risk profiles that define construction and real estate operations in Kazakhstan.

How We Help

  • Tax Planning for Construction Projects — Structuring projects to optimize tax positions across multi-year development timelines, joint ventures, and phased investments
  • Project Finance Advisory — Advising on financing structures, grant mechanisms, and debt arrangements with favorable tax treatment
  • VAT Optimization for Construction Companies — Managing VAT recovery on capital expenditures, input credits, and complex subcontractor chains
  • Land Use & Property Tax Compliance — Ensuring compliance with land allocation rules, property tax assessments, and municipal levies
  • Tax Incentive Structures for Development Zones — Securing and maintaining eligibility for special economic zone benefits, free warehouse zones, and regional incentive programs
  • Audit Services for Construction Companies — Statutory and IFRS audits tailored to the construction industry, including percentage-of-completion accounting and contract revenue recognition
  • Labor Law Compliance for Construction Workforce — Navigating employment regulations, foreign worker permits, and payroll obligations for large project teams

Key Regulatory Knowledge

Effective advisory in construction and real estate requires deep familiarity with the regulatory frameworks that govern the sector. Our team maintains current expertise in:

  • Construction Licensing Requirements — License categories, qualification requirements, and compliance procedures for general contractors and specialized firms
  • Special Economic Zone Tax Benefits — Corporate income tax exemptions, land tax preferences, and VAT benefits available in designated development zones
  • Property Tax Rates & Exemptions — Assessment methodologies, applicable rates for different property classes, and available exemptions for new construction
  • Land Code Provisions — Land allocation procedures, lease terms, change-of-use regulations, and ownership rights for domestic and foreign entities
  • Public Procurement Rules for State-Funded Projects — Tender procedures, documentation requirements, and compliance obligations for contractors working on government-funded developments

Why MinTax

MinTax Group brings practical, hands-on experience with residential, commercial, and infrastructure projects across Kazakhstan. Our advisors have worked with developers on large-scale housing programs, commercial office and retail developments, industrial parks, and public infrastructure projects.

We combine this sector-specific knowledge with our core strengths in tax, audit, and legal services to deliver integrated solutions that address the real challenges construction and real estate businesses face — from project planning through completion and ongoing asset management.
