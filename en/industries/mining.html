Mining & Metals

Expert advisory for Kazakhstan's extractive industries. From resource taxation and environmental compliance to cross-border structuring and IFRS reporting, we support mining operations at every stage.

Sector Overview

Kazakhstan ranks among the world's top producers of uranium, chromium, copper, and zinc, with significant reserves of manganese, lead, gold, and rare earth elements. The mining and metals sector is a critical pillar of the national economy, attracting substantial domestic and foreign investment.

Mining operations in Kazakhstan face unique tax, environmental, and regulatory challenges that require specialized knowledge of the Subsoil Use Code, mineral extraction tax calculations, and environmental remediation obligations. MinTax brings deep, hands-on experience with major mining operations across the country's mineral-rich regions.

Our Services for Mining & Metals

  • Resource Taxation Advisory — Strategic advice on mineral extraction tax calculations, excess profit tax provisions, and tax optimization for exploration, development, and production phases
  • Environmental Reporting & Compliance — Assistance with environmental impact assessments, emissions reporting, remediation planning, and compliance with the Environmental Code
  • Cross-Border Operations Structuring — Designing tax-efficient structures for multinational mining groups operating in Kazakhstan, including holding company planning and financing arrangements
  • Compliance Audits for Mining Licenses — Verification of compliance with subsoil use contract terms, license conditions, and mandatory investment obligations
  • IFRS Reporting for Extractive Industries — Implementation of IFRS 6 (Exploration for and Evaluation of Mineral Resources) and related standards for accurate financial reporting
  • Transfer Pricing for Mineral Exports — Documentation, benchmarking, and defense of intercompany pricing for mineral concentrate and refined metal exports

Key Regulatory Knowledge

Our professionals maintain current expertise in the regulatory framework governing Kazakhstan's mining sector:

  • Subsoil Use Code provisions for solid minerals exploration and extraction
  • Mineral extraction tax calculation methodologies and rates
  • Environmental remediation requirements and financial provisioning
  • Geological information reporting and state geological fund submissions
  • Kazakh content requirements for goods, works, and services
  • Customs regulations for mining equipment imports and mineral exports
  • Labor and safety regulations specific to mining operations
