Oil & Gas

Comprehensive tax, audit, and advisory services for Kazakhstan's energy sector. 26+ years of experience navigating petroleum tax regimes, subsoil use agreements, and cross-border energy operations.

Sector Overview

Kazakhstan's oil and gas sector is one of the largest in Central Asia, anchored by major fields such as Tengiz, Kashagan, and Karachaganak. The industry operates within a complex framework of subsoil use agreements, production sharing agreements, and a specialized petroleum tax regime that demands deep technical knowledge.

MinTax has served energy sector clients since our founding, building unmatched expertise in the regulatory, tax, and financial reporting challenges unique to exploration, production, transportation, and refining operations across Kazakhstan.

Our Services for Oil & Gas

  • Tax Advisory for Exploration & Production — Structuring operations to optimize tax positions under Kazakhstan's petroleum tax regime, including mineral extraction tax, excess profit tax, and export duties
  • Subsoil Use Agreement Compliance — Ensuring full compliance with contractual obligations, reporting requirements, and fiscal stability provisions within subsoil use contracts
  • Transfer Pricing for Cross-Border Transactions — Documentation, benchmarking, and dispute resolution for intercompany transactions in energy supply chains
  • Environmental Compliance & Reporting — Advisory on environmental fees, emissions reporting, and compliance with Kazakhstan's Environmental Code
  • Customs & Trade Advisory — Managing customs duties, temporary import regimes, and trade compliance for equipment and materials used in upstream and midstream operations
  • Audit Under ISA & IFRS — Independent audits of oil and gas companies in accordance with International Standards on Auditing and IFRS reporting frameworks

Key Regulatory Knowledge

Our team maintains deep expertise in the legislative and regulatory framework governing Kazakhstan's energy sector:

  • Subsoil Use Code of the Republic of Kazakhstan
  • Environmental Code and environmental fee calculations
  • Tax treatment of production sharing agreements (PSAs)
  • Excess profit tax provisions and calculation methodologies
  • Mineral extraction tax rates and compliance requirements
  • Export customs duties on crude oil and petroleum products
  • Transfer pricing rules for cross-border energy transactions
