About MinTax Group

For over 25 years, we've helped Kazakhstan's businesses navigate complexity with confidence. We combine international standards with deep local expertise.

Built on Trust, Driven by Excellence

MinTax Group was founded in 1999 by professionals who had helped shape Kazakhstan's tax system. We believed businesses deserved advisors who understood both the letter of the law and the practical realities of operating in a rapidly evolving economy.

That founding principle remains our guide today. We don't just advise on compliance — we help clients build structures that work, anticipate challenges before they arise, and defend positions when they're tested.

Our Story

1999

MinTax LLP Founded

Established in Almaty by professionals from Kazakhstan's Ministry of Finance. The name "MinTax" reflects both our mission — helping clients minimize tax burden legally — and our founders' institutional heritage.

2001

MinTax Audit Launched

Expanded into audit services with the establishment of MinTax Audit LLP, obtaining a state license from the Ministry of Finance.

2010

KASE Recognition

Included on the Kazakhstan Stock Exchange list of recognized audit organizations, enabling us to serve listed companies.

2011

Legal Practice Expanded

Established close cooperation with Premium Solutions law firm, adding licensed advocacy capabilities for court representation.

2016

National Recognition

Named "Best Tax Consultants' Organization" by the Chamber of Tax Consultants and Atameken National Chamber of Entrepreneurs.

Today

Continuing to Grow

Serving clients across Kazakhstan from offices in Almaty, Astana, and Atyrau. Consistently ranked among Kazakhstan's top 10 audit and consulting firms.

Our Structure

MinTax Group comprises three integrated practices:

MinTax LLP

Tax and legal consulting services. Our founding entity, established 1999.

MinTax Audit LLP

Audit and assurance services. Licensed by the Ministry of Finance, KASE-recognized.

Premium Solutions

Affiliated law firm providing licensed advocacy and court representation.

This structure allows us to serve clients comprehensively while maintaining the independence required for audit engagements. A partnership agreement unites the group while each entity maintains its own professional responsibilities.
